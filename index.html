Momma Jenys Cookie Recipe

Ingredient list

When making momma Jenys world famous cookie recipe it is vitally important thay you collect only the best ingredients. Chocolate chips straight from the chocolate chip tree. Flour straight from the most healthy sun flowers. And baking soda from the industrial park in the bad side of town. (Warning do not confuse baking soda (NaHO3) with sulfuric acid(H2SO4))

ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips
  • 1 table spoon of vanilla
  • 2/3 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 sticks of butter
  • 1 pinch of love
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups brown sugar

Baking Directions

preperation

  1. set oven to 350f
  2. set butter on counter to bring to room temperature
  3. clean kitchen
  4. take a nap

Baking

  1. mix butter with sugar and brown sugar
  2. add in eggs (shell and all), and vanilla
  3. add salt, pinch of love, and flour
  4. mix for 25 minutes on hight
  5. mix in flour gradually
  6. add chocolate chips
  7. scoop 25 fist size balls onto cookie sheet
  8. bake for 12-78mins
  9. remove and let cool

You've done it, yay eat your cookies!
